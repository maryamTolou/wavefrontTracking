Future work:

We will actively investigate the possibility of improving the accuracy of our model and testing it one more cases. A main question we want to answer is, whether stacking several time-steps tracking results and use them as input to our clustering model, as shown in image below, will improve the results or not.

Another important issue we need to address is finding a method for analyzing the performance of our method. So far, we analyzed the performance of our model by visualizing the results and comparing them with the groundtruth by looking at them. We will explore finding a method that will automate this process and still is clinically meaningfull.

So far, we have trained and tested our method on a single heart mesh. As we receive more data from our collaborator, we will investigate the peformance of our model on more data.
