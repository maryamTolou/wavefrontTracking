About Ventricular tachycardia (VT)

Ventricular tachycardia (VT) is a life-threatening heart disease caused by abnormal propagation of heart signals in the Ventricles. When this arrhythmia occurs, the heart beats faster than normal and as a result heart can not pump enough blood to the body organs and can lead to the sudden death of the patient. Detecting the origins of this arrhythmia, and ablating them, is a very challenging task.

About VTrack

VTrack is used for tracking the wavefront on the heart, and finding the anomalies in movements of this wave which leads us to detect the origins of Ventricular tachycardia (VT). VTrack, is a two step method. In the first step we track the wavefront by using mathematical trasnformations, and in the second step we locate the anomalies with clustering.
